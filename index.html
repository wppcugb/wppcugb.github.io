Short Bio

I am currently an Associate Professor at China University of Geosciences, Beijing (CUGB).

I obtained my Ph.D. degree from School of Information Engineering, China University of Geosciences, Beijing in 2023, under the supervision of Prof. Xinqi Zheng, Foreign academician of the Russian Academy of Natural Sciences.

My research interests mainly focus on spatiotemporal data mining, geographic health analysis and modeling, as well as urban network resilience modeling. I aim to develop complex network models that characterize interactions between cities to support resilience assessment of metropolitan regions and enhance regional resilience, especially under disruptions such as COVID-19, providing decision-making basis for urban planning and sustainable development.
